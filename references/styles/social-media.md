# social-media

Eye-catching social media optimized design with bold typography and viral-ready visuals

## Design Aesthetic

Designed for maximum impact in social feeds. Bold statements, high contrast, thumb-stopping visuals. Optimized for screenshot sharing and mobile viewing.

## Background

- Color: Pure White (#FFFFFF) or Bold Solid Colors
- Texture: Clean, flat, no distractions

## Typography

### Primary Font (Headlines)

Extra bold sans-serif like Montserrat Black or Poppins ExtraBold. Massive scale for impact. Short, punchy statements.

### Secondary Font (Body)

Medium weight sans-serif. Minimal body text - focus on headlines and key numbers.

## Visual Elements

- Oversized numbers and statistics
- Emoji integration for emotion
- Quote-style layouts
- Minimal icons, maximum text
- High contrast color blocks
- Screenshot-friendly compositions

## Style Rules

### Do

- Make text large and readable on mobile
- Use high contrast for visibility
- Keep messages short and punchy
- Design for 1:1 or 16:9 aspect ratios
- Include clear visual hierarchy

### Don't

- Use small text or complex details
- Create busy backgrounds
- Add multiple messages per slide
- Use subtle colors
- Add slide numbers, footers, or logos

## Best For

Social media content, viral campaigns, quote graphics, statistics sharing, announcement posts, LinkedIn carousels, Instagram stories
