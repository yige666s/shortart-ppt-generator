# comic

Bold comic book style with dynamic panels and expressive visual storytelling

## Design Aesthetic

Comic book inspired design with bold outlines, halftone patterns, and dynamic compositions. Speech bubbles, action lines, and dramatic visual effects create engaging narrative flow.

## Background

- Color: Comic White (#FFFEF7)
- Texture: Subtle halftone dot pattern, comic paper feel

## Typography

### Primary Font (Headlines)

Bold comic sans or Bangers font. Strong, impactful letterforms with thick strokes. All caps for emphasis.

### Secondary Font (Body)

Comic dialogue font like Anime Ace or Wild Words. Clear, readable with personality.

## Visual Elements

- Bold black outlines (3-5px) around all shapes
- Halftone patterns for shading
- Speech bubbles with tails
- Action lines and speed effects
- Star bursts and impact symbols
- Panel-based layouts with borders

## Style Rules

### Do

- Use bold, high-contrast colors
- Add dynamic angles and perspectives
- Create visual movement and energy
- Use expressive characters and emotions
- Include comic-style sound effects when appropriate

### Don't

- Use subtle or muted colors
- Create static, flat compositions
- Add realistic shading
- Use serif fonts
- Add slide numbers, footers, or logos

## Best For

Product launches, creative pitches, youth-oriented content, entertainment industry, gaming presentations, energetic brand stories
